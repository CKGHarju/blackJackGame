BLACKJACK
Press play to start!
Rules: Win by having a higher hand than the dealer, if your hand is over 21 you bust! Dealer draws new cards untill it has 17 or more. Ace is worth 1 or 11, picture cards are worth 10, and rest of the cards are worth their value. BlackJack is achieved by getting an Ace and a picture card, it'll win over any other hand.
